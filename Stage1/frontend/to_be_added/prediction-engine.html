The training loop

Five steps, every scenario.

STEP 01

Pick a variant

Each scenario ships with multiple datasets — including ones uploaded by your class.

Review the data

See the dataset as a scatter plot, plus a bias check that flags sensitive columns.

STEP 03

Train the model

Watch an animated data packet fly into the training loop. Async Celery runs the real model.

Read the explanation

Every result ships with a 5-section Q&A: why this model, what was fed, can you fool it, and more.

Predict live
